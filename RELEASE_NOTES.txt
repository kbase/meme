OVERVIEW
-----------------------------------------
	This  KBase service integrate capabilities of three MEME Suite tools:
	- MEME tool for finding ungapped motifs in unaligned DNA sequences
	- TOMTOM tool for comparison of an input DNA motif to the elements of a database of known motifs
	- MAST tool for searching biological sequence databases for occurrences of known motifs
